# 🚀 Overview

**GENESIS X** is a cinematic futuristic landing page inspired by artificial intelligence, cybernetics, and immersive storytelling.

Rather than presenting information like a conventional website, Genesis X guides visitors through an interactive onboarding experience where they become candidates for the next stage of human evolution.

Every animation, transition, and interaction contributes to a seamless science-fiction narrative.

---

# 🌌 Story

> **Year 2098**

Human civilization has nearly vanished.

The world's last superintelligent AI, **GENESIS X**, has begun rebuilding humanity through cybernetic evolution.

You are among the first selected candidates.

Your journey begins now.
